About

NCA

North Coast Aquatics is the largest competitive swim team in San Diego County. Over the past 34 years NCA has helped place over 240 of its swimmers into major colleges across the country, and has been the highest achieving swim club in San Diego County for the last 2 decades. NCA swimmers form lifelong friendships with their teammates and competitors. We hope that your stay with our team, however short or long term, will be enjoyable and rewarding.
