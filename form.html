Thank-you for your interest in joining North Coast Aquatics.

This page will give you important information about some of our membership requirements so that you'll have a better idea of what's involved in joining our club.

Membership Fees

Membership fees vary by groups and number of days of per week. Some additional discounts can be applied based on criteria that the tryout coordinator can discuss with you at the time of your tryout. The following are some approximate price ranges for our groups.

  • 10&U: $110 - $173/month
  • 11-12: $195/month
  • 13-18: $120 - $204/month
More detailed information about our billing structure can be found HERE.

Time Commitments

Being part of a competitive swim team, like any group or club, has some time commitment requirements. Times and days vary by group, starting with 2 days per week of 60 minute sessions for our younger athletes, going all the way up to 9 workouts per week for our senior athletes. Carlsbad Alga Norte schedules are here and La Jolla JCC Schedules are here.

Team Equipment

Being part of a team is a lot of fun. At North Coast, we pride ourselves on being a team and looking like a team. To do so, we have some outfiting and equipment requirements. These vary depending on which group you are in. More information on the requirements for each group can be found here.

USA Swimming Membership

As a USA Swimming Club, NCA requires that all it's members join USA Swimming. Once you are part of NCA, we will provide you with more information to guide you as to how to applying for a USA Swimming membership.
