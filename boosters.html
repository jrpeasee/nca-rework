North Coast Aquatics Boosters



The NCA Boosters is a 501(C)3 nonprofit organization (Tax ID 95-3825957) made up of North Coast Aquatics parents. The NCA Boosters Board is comprised of a President, Past President, Vice President, Treasurer, Secretary, five members at large and Head Coach Jeff Pease. The primary function of the NCA Boosters is to raise money to support swimmers and coaches. The fund-raising dollars go to support team travel and to provide for awards, championship t-shirts & caps, educational clinics & seminars, equipment, swim fee scholarships and team socials.

Major events and activities that the NCA Boosters have sponsored are: Holiday party, JO t-shirts and caps, Annual Awards presentation (helps to subsidize), Relay event, Coaches education, Needs at sites - bins for equipment, pace clocks, kick boards, etc., Amazing Quest and other swimmer incentives, Swimmer of the Month awards

The above activities and events are organized and hosted by NCA Boosters. They take a great deal of work and organization from not only the NCA Booster Board, but from all the parents and families who's swimmers are members

Message from the North Coast Aquatics Boosters



Did you know that Individual and Corporate Donations to the North Coast Aquatics Boosters are tax deductible? Below are examples of how NCA Team Members have taken advantage of this:

Donate on an individual basis. Donate through your employer-sponsored United Way/CHAD campaign by designating the North Coast Aquatics Boosters as the "qualified" charitable organization. Donate on an Individual basis (employee) and have donation matched by company (employer). Solicit a donation from a company that sponsors youth sports organizations.

The North Coast Aquatics Boosters' goal is to provide financial assistance through fund-raising to benefit team travel, the Kay Pentz scholarship fund for college-bound athletes, a scholarship program for swimmers who experience financial hardships, awards, and team equipment and functions. Want to know more? Please contact Mary Kay Barry at mkbkmb@yahoo.com.
